About

My career at Industrial Traffic / 4Walls Media Group started as a Search Engine Optimization (SEO) specialist and Video Content Producer. While working with a cross-functional team, I brought existing skills from my past-career in Television and News Production to overhaul the use of video and photography in digital campaigns. As a developer, having a stake in the both the creative and development process early in my career was invaluable. Today, I enjoy using every technology available to collaborate with teams to make the delivery of digital assets faster and more user-friendly.

What I'm Proud of / Career highlights / Times I Patted Myself On the Back

  • Introduced and implemented responsive design frameworks company-wide for new and existing clients.
  • Drastically reduced load-times across customer portfolio as part of ongoing optimization and SEO efforts | technologies used: Grunt , Gulp , Tinypng , Above the Fold CSS and async loading etc.
  • Concepted & created video & photo assets for use on the web and in integrated marketing campaigns.
  • Collaborated with development teams to launch a Hitachi division website while carefully abiding by the strict corporate guidelines of a global conglomerate.
