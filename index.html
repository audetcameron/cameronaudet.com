About

My career at Industrial Traffic / 4Walls Media Group started as a Search Engine Optimization (SEO) specialist and Video Content Producer. While working with a cross-functional team, I brought existing skills from my past-career in Television and News Production to overhaul the use of video and photography in digital campaigns. As a developer, having a stake in the both the creative and development process early in my career was invaluable. Today, I enjoy using every technology available to collaborate with teams to make the delivery of digital assets faster and more user-friendly.

What I'm Proud of / Career highlights / Times I Patted Myself On the Back

  • Introduced and implemented responsive design frameworks company wide for new and existing clients.
  • Drastically reduced load-times across customer portfolio as part of ongoing optimization and SEO efforts | technologies used: Grunt , Gulp , Tinypng , Above the Fold CSS and async loading etc.
  • Concepted & created video & photo assets for use on the web and in integrated marketing campaigns.
  • Collaborated with development teams to launch Hitachi Cable America Division’s website while carefully abiding by the strict corporate guidelines of a global conglomerate.

My Core Strengths

Creating and maintaining multiple websites that fit modern standards in UX/UI and code optimization using a LAMP environment. This includes: Responsive Static Websites, E-Commerce Solutions and Wordpress / Drupal CMS.

What I'm learning now

I have a passion and desire to grow as a developer. I see my next step in my career using new front-end application frameworks and have been learning NPM, React, React Native, Vuejs, Angular, Node, Express, Mongo, and build tools like grunt, gulp and most recently webpack to include technologies like babel.

My End Goal

I would like to advance in my career where I can practically incorporate these new technologies into my everyday tasks as part of a team producing large scale single page applications or mobile experiences. I would also like to become more familiar with Dev Operations like Aws, Docker, Vagrant and Cloud Computing.
